    SoftMouse.NET Release Notes - Latest release v 5.5.10 on April 11, 2025   

    We are continually improving SoftMouse to make it an even more valuable animal colony database for our customers.
    Feel free to use the built-in Suggestions form (from gear at the top right) and let us know your thoughts - we'll do our best to incorporate your thoughts into upcoming versions of SoftMouse.NET.

    • SoftMouse.NET v 5.5.10 on April 11, 2025
    • Past Releases
    New Colony Features:
    1. Improved cage list loading time: We've optimized the cage list to load more quickly, so you can access your data faster and stay focused on what matters most.

    Fixes:
    1. Cage cards language: Printed cage cards now reflect your preferred language.
